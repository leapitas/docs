# About Leap Sign
Leap Sign enables electronic signatures directly within Business Central. The connector is compatible with third-party signature solutions such as Visma Sign.

## Key Features

- **Effortless Integration**: Integrate e-signature capabilities into Business Central
- **Process Monitoring**: Initiate the signature process and monitor its progress directly within Business Central
- **Multi-Document Signing**: Simultaneously sign multiple sales documents, contracts, and other essential documents
- **Document Retrieval**: Retrieve the signed documents directly from Business Central whenever needed
- **User-Friendly Interface**: Provide an intuitive and user-friendly interface for a smooth signing experience
- **Quick Onboarding Process**: Fast setup and configuration
