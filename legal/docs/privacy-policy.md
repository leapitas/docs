# Privacy Policy

This privacy policy applies to all the services and applications used and delivered by Leap IT AS. Please contact us for any questions.

## What information do we collect

- **Name, address and billing information** of our customers and suppliers
- **Persons and companies** who contact us for sales and support might be registered
- **Telemetric data** from our apps. Such data is not personal identifiable and will only be used to log errors and for debug purposes
- **Cookies** from our website

## How do we obtain this information

- **ERP system**: All information registered by us is stored in our ERP system, Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central
- **Telemetric data**: Obtained by Microsoft Azure services, Microsoft 365 services or by our own apps/code
- **Cookies**: Obtained from our website powered by Squarespace. Refer to [http://www.aboutcookies.org](http://www.aboutcookies.org) for more information about cookies

## How do we store collected data

All information collected by us are stored in services provided by Microsoft. Please refer to the privacy policy of Microsoft Azure, Microsoft 365 and Dynamics 365 Business Central for updated information.

## What we use

We might use registered personal and company information regarding business matters like sales, support and advertisement.

This page will be updated with our current privacy policy.
