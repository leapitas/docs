# Install in Visma App Store
To complete the integration, Leap Visma Employees Import must also be installed and configured in Visma App Store.

## Installation Steps
### 1. Log in to Visma App Store
- Go to [apps.visma.com](https://apps.visma.com) and log in with the same credentials you use in Visma.net
- Alternatively: Go to Visma Home and click on "App Store"

:::warning App Store not available?
If Visma App Store is not available, see [guide to activate Visma App Store](activate-app-store.md).
:::

### 2. Find and install the app
1. **Search for** "Leap Visma Employees Import" and click on the application
2. **Click on** "Grant permissions"
3. **Review the data types** that the application wants to retrieve from Visma Employees

### 3. Approve permissions
You will now see which types of data this application wants to retrieve from Visma Employees.

**By clicking "Integrate"** you approve that the application gets access to the relevant data. This is a prerequisite for reading data from Visma Employees to Business Central.

### 4. Confirm installation
When you have clicked "Integrate", a message will be sent to Leap IT AS that your company wants to use the application. It is now ready to connect Visma Employees to Dynamics Business Central.

## Find your Tenant ID
**Important**: You need the Tenant ID to complete the setup in Business Central.
1. **In the upper right corner** in Visma, click on the dropdown icon
2. **Copy the value** in "Tenant ID"
3. **Save this** for later, as it will be used to connect Business Central to Visma Employees
