# About Leap Visma Employees Import
Leap Visma Employees Import updates employees and resources in Dynamics 365 Business Central from Visma Employees. All communication happens via API connection between Business Central and Visma Employees.

## Key Functionality

- **Direct integration**: Integrates directly with Visma Employees via Visma's API
- **Simple setup**: Easy and quick setup
- **Employee administration**: Create and update employees in Business Central with data from Visma Employees
- **Resource administration**: Create and update resources in Business Central with data from Visma Employees
- **Dimension support**: Support for importing dimensions on employees and resources
- **Cost calculation**: Ability to calculate unit cost on resources in Business Central
- **SSB integration**: Import of job codes and job titles from Statistics Norway
