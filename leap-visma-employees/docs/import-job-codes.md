# Import job codes from Statistics Norway
If you want to use job codes from Statistics Norway linked to the employees, this is imported directly from SSB to Business Central. Then the employees will get updated job descriptions based on the job code in Visma.

## Storage in Business Central
When you import these values, they are stored in the standard table **"Employee Statistics Groups"** in Business Central.

## Perform import of job codes

### From the employee list
1. **Open** the employee list in Business Central
2. **Select** "Import employee statistics groups" from the menu
3. **The import** starts automatically and retrieves data from SSB
