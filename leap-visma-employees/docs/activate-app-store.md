# Activate App Store in Visma
Leap Visma Employees Import is available on Visma's marketplace "Visma App Store". If you don't have Visma App Store available as shown in the steps below, you must contact Visma or your Visma partner for help.

## Activation via Visma.net Admin
### 1. Log in to Visma.net Admin
Start by logging in to Visma.net Admin with administrator access.
### 2. Find the correct customer and company
Find the correct customer and the company you want to use the integration with.
### 3. Activate the App Store product
Activate the product **"App Store"** under services.
### 4. Assign the correct roles
1. **Go to** "Users and Roles"
2. **Find the user** who should be able to activate integrations
3. **Assign the role** "Integration Administrator"

### 5. Verify access
The user can now:
- **Log in** via [apps.visma.com](https://apps.visma.com)
- **See App Store** as a separate menu option the next time they log in to Visma Home

## Visma App Store available in Visma Home
When activation is complete, App Store will be visible as a separate menu option in Visma Home.
