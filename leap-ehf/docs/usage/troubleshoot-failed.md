# Troubleshooting Failed Documents

If a document fails, this will be visible in several places in the system:

- **Posted Sales Invoice and Posted Sales Credit Memo lists**: E-Document Status will then be set to **Error**. You can click in the column to open the e-document card and see the error message
- **Tile on Role Center**: You can click on the tile and get a list of E-Documents that have failed
- **E-Documents**: Search for **E-Documents** in the search menu and click on the corresponding link. E-Documents will be marked with "**Error**" in the "**Electronic Document Status**" column
