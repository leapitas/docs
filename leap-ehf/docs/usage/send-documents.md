# Send Documents as EHF

When you now post documents that result in either a posted sales invoice, posted sales credit memo, posted service invoice, or posted service credit memo, EHF will automatically be sent to the customer.

This will happen regardless of whether you choose **Post**, or **Post and Send**.

If you open the "**Posted Sales Invoices**" list, or "**Posted Sales Credit Memos**" list, you will see a separate column "**E-Document Status**".
