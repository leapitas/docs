# Welcome to Leap IT Documentation

Comprehensive documentation for all Leap IT products and services designed to integrate seamlessly with Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central.

## Our Products

### Leap Sign
Digital signature solution for Business Central that enables secure, legally binding document signing directly from your ERP system. Perfect for sales documents, contracts, and agreements.

### Leap Visma Employees Import
Automated employee data synchronization between Visma HRM systems and Business Central. Eliminate manual data entry and maintain consistent employee records across platforms.

### Leap Visma Payroll Import
Complete payroll integration solution that automatically imports payroll transactions from Visma Payroll into Business Central with full financial posting and account mapping.
